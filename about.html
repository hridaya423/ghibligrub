Our Story 🌸

✨ Our Humble Beginnings

Ghibli Grub began as a dream in the heart of our founder, Mei Kusakabe, who grew up enchanted by Studio Ghibli films. What started as weekend pop-ups at local farmers' markets has blossomed into our cozy restaurant, where the magic of animation meets culinary artistry. Each dish we serve carries memories of those early days when we first dared to bring fictional feasts into reality.

🎬 Our Ghibli Inspiration

From the savory ramen of Ponyo to the mysterious tea served in Spirited Away, our menu celebrates the heartwarming meals that have become iconic in Studio Ghibli's cinematic worlds. We've studied every frame featuring food with the dedication of true fans, consulting with Japanese culinary experts to ensure authenticity while adding our own touch of whimsy and wonder to each plate.

🌱 Our Culinary Philosophy

At Ghibli Grub, we believe food should nourish both body and spirit. We source ingredients locally and seasonally, honoring the deep connection to nature that permeates Ghibli films. Our kitchen operates with zero waste principles, transforming scraps into stocks and composting what remains. Like Totoro's forest spirits, we seek to protect and celebrate the natural world through every meal we serve.
